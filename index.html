Frequently Asked Questions

Event design and event planning work hand in hand to deliver a successful event.Events by Knight offer tailor made events. This means we incorporate both event design and event planning to create that perfect event.

It is important to start planning an event as early as possible, ideally several months in advance, especially if it is a large or complex event. This allows time to secure a venue, vendors, and any necessary permits or licenses, as well as to coordinate schedules and create a detailed timeline.

It is important to review the specific policy of the organization or service you are dealing with as it may vary depending on the industry or context. It is always advisable to understand the terms and conditions related to refunds and cancellations before making a purchase or commitment to avoid any potential disputes.

To keep budgets on track, it is important to set a realistic budget from the outset and create a detailed plan that includes all potential expenses. Regular monitoring and tracking of expenses against the budget should be done throughout the planning process, and adjustments should be made as necessary to stay on track.
